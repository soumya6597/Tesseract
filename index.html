TESSERACT

Put your coding skills to work, let your entrepreneurial spirit rise because we are here with Innerve's Tesseract. Tesseract is a machine learning and artificial intelligence based hackathon which encourages participants to become problem solvers of tomorrow. For round 1, there are 6 verses in the Tesseract multiverse, namely, Health, Finance, Education, Agriculture, Robotics, and Environment, from which the teams have to choose the one that intrigues them most! Each verse aims to address a problem that is often overlooked but is essential to solve, with the use of ML and AI. Amidst algorithms and codes, we are looking for participants who are not only developers, but brilliant minds who are industry ready. And with that we have our final round, where participants are required to present their designed products along with their revenue models in front of a panel of judges in the final round. But this is not your average hackathon, stay tuned to know why!

Environment

The environment today is on the brink of total anarchy. From global warming to UV exposures, it is all a domino effect. In various inventions today, existing toxins of the environment are not eliminated and this leads only to generation of further toxins.

The second largest populated country in the world, India faces various hindrances to its development. Waste Management is of critical concern and needs attention. Whereas many developed countries are searching for ready-made sustainable waste management solutions. The wastes have become a major consequence of development, modernization and urbanization. waste includes biodegradable, non biodegradable, Electronic waste. Electronic waste affects the natural environment to a great extent and poses a serious threat as these materials remain in one particular place or a relatively longer period of time unless removed or burned away. In this respect, electronic waste has been identified as a significant matter for sustainable development. Growth in population and change in lifestyle has led to an increase in waste generation. Both the residential and commercial waste are in biodegradable, non biodegradable, electronic form. Safe and cost-effective waste management is a great challenge for modern society. It is estimated that 40% of the waste remains uncollected. This research aims for a technical solution for the management of wastes by providing visibility of AI. In this problem we have to develop a robotic dustbin using AI in which there are three subsections. Design it in such a way that it detects the nature of the waste itself and bifurcates it.

Education

The education verse, for us, holds a special place. It is a verse which includes providing access to education to all, along with improving the quality of education, faculty, infrastructure etc. However is the typical system enough? Or is there something being overlooked?

Ever Wondered how the world would be a different place if everybody could communicate effectively . Communication is not just a medium to exchange ideas but also a very important tool for education . Not everybody communicates the same way , As per WHO about 63 million people in India are deaf and about 7.45 % of the total disabled population in India has speech impairment . So the proportion of the population which uses sign language is also considerable .The main concern of using Sign Language is that it can not be understood by everyone , which hampers effectiveness while communicating . To aid this the participants have to come up with unique ideas to use the appropriate technology to build a 'Sign Language Translator'. The translator should enhance the accessibility and usability of people with speech disabilities . Apart from this function it should be user friendly as well. Not only would it enhance learning but may also increase job opportunities.

Finance

Finance is a verse which is a crucial component of all other verses. It includes various financial services being provided such as banking, money exchange, investments etc. But we want participants to look beyond the ordinary. We aim to let participants enter into the realm of fintech to further boost the abilities of this verse.

The purpose of creating a mobile app to examine all the purchases made by scanning a receipt is to assist consumers in viewing and studying their overall expenditure analysis. Simply take a picture of a receipt and use optical character recognition (OCR) to track payments. The software should be able to categorize products using AI and machine learning, such as food, clothing, fuel, etc. When customers spend too much money or make repeated transactions, the app should alert them and it displays your payment receipts for credits and debits as well as a monthly graph. ####To help customers evaluate and analyze their overall spending, a mobile app was developed that allows them to scan a receipt to view all the purchases made. Track payments by simply taking a picture of a receipt and using optical character recognition (OCR). Using AI and machine learning, the software should be able to classify objects like food, clothing, fuel, and other items. The software should notify users when they make too frequent or expensive purchases. It also shows monthly graphs and payment receipts for credits and debits.

Knowing the right soil type, fertility, hygiene, water content, requirement of fertilizers, can save a lot of effort and money for farmers. About 60% percent of people have a keen interest in gardening but with increasing workload and time scarcity, having one's own garden is becoming a privilege. Aim to develop a device which can help people in growing the plant species which will be suitable for them based on their location, requirements, and housing conditions.

Agriculture

In recent times there has been great development in agriculture in terms of AI but still in India most farmers are from rural areas and are very less educated so they are not able to get full advantage of technology. So our problem statement involves making an app with a voice assistant that can build a solution to help farmers view their own accounts and manage their business better, and to provide training in how to use technology in an interesting manner and also use AR/VR to give live demos of technologies used in farms.
